About Pattern Sense

Pattern Sense is a fabric pattern classification web app powered by deep learning. It uses state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks to recognize and classify textile designs from user-uploaded images.

This tool is useful for textile researchers, fashion designers, and fabric enthusiasts who wish to quickly identify styles like floral, striped, dotted, paisley, and more.
